Why must have this app?

Pdowin is an educational platform that conducts online general knowledge study tests and provides scholarships for winner users. In the General Knowledge study test, questions up to class 10th standard are included which are taken from different subjects. This general knowledge study test can be given by anyone who has knowledge of studies. In this test, the rank of users is determined and scholarship is distributed based on their rank. Pdowin website (pdowin.com) and pdowin app & pdowin social media accounts together are called pdowin platform. Pdowin platform inspires students to run startups with new ideas within the country. The Pdowin platform provides encouragement to skill based innovations. Pdowin platform always works with new ideas to support students.
